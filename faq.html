Frequently Asked Questions

What is syncNscan?

syncNscan is a cloud based backup, anti-theft security software and insurance service. syncNscan stores all your phone contacts, call logs, SMS, calendar and photos on syncNscan cloud. If you lose your phone, syncNscan can help you find your phone, remotely activate the alarm, lock your phone and wipe your personal data. All of your backed up data can be restored to another mobile device.

What languages does syncNscan support?

Currently syncNscan is only available in English.

syncNscan will soon be available in the following languages:
• Arabic
• Bahasa
• Chinese
• German
• Korean
• Portugese
• Russian
• Spanish
• Thai

If you would like syncNscan available in other languages, please contact support@syncnscan.com

What operating systems does syncNscan currently run on?

syncNscan currently runs on phones running Android 2.2, 2.3 and Android 4.

Is this service available worldwide?

Yes, the service is available worldwide.

What if I forget my PIN?

Click on Forgot PIN on your phone. Your main trusted friend will receive a message with a temporary PIN to assist you.

How do I contact syncNscan for support?

For support, please email support@syncnscan.com

How do I report errors in the application to the syncNscan team?

To report an error, please email support@syncnscan.com. Provide detailed information by choosing Settings and then Send Log from syncNscan to help further.

Are there any limitations to the size of my backup?

Currently, there are no limitations to the size of your backup except that we will enforce a fair use policy in case of misuse.

How secure is my data?

Your data is securely stored and encrypted on the server using military grade encryption technologies.

How does syncNscan affect battery life?

syncNscan has minimal impact on battery life as observed in phones worldwide. However, if you do see any impact on battery life as a result of installing the application, please email us at support@syncnscan.com

Can I use syncNscan without a data connection?

No, syncNscan uses GPRS, 3G or Wi-Fi connections to connect to the syncNscan cloud service.>

Can I use syncNscan on a tablet (Android or iPad)?

No. syncNscan has been specifically designed to operate on mobile phone devices. Tablet devices are part of our roadmap.

Can I use syncNscan without a SIM (subscriber identity module)?

A SIM is required to identify the mobile user and log on to the syncNscan cloud service.

Why do I need to provide the number of a Trusted friend?

Your phone number is required so when your phone is lost or stolen: • Trusted friends are automatically informed if the SIM is changed
• Trusted friends can remotely locate your phone
• Trusted friends can remotely activate an alarm on your phone
• Trusted friends can remotely lock your phone and wipe your data

Are videos, sounds, music backed up with syncNscan?

At the moment syncNscan is focussed on backing up a common range of items across a broad range of phones such as messages, contacts, call logs, images and calendar. We will soon support more file types.

Does syncNscan backup MMS'?

No, syncNscan does not backup MMS.

Why is the application named syncNscan?

The name has been derived from amalgamation of two key features provided by the application – Sync (to backup your data) and Scan (to detect and eliminate threats on your phone)

Do I need a computer to use syncNscan?

A computer is not required to use syncNscan The information on the syncNscan cloud can be viewed using the browser on your phone.

Is a site license available for syncNscan?

As per the terms of service syncNscan is currently licensed to individual users. However, if you need an enterprise version of this software, please email us at support@syncnscan.com.
